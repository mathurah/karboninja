OUR MISSION

KarboNinja predicts a car’s CO2 emissions to make information easily accessible for car manufacturers and also make them aware of their carbon footprint. Car’s emit high amounts of carbon emissions per gallon which increases our carbon footprint. To combat that problem, KarboNinja predicts the carbon emissions depending on certain specs to help companies track their carbon footprints.
